About Fitness Superstore

Our Story

Founded in 2005, Fitness Superstore began with a simple mission: to make high-quality fitness equipment accessible to everyone. What started as a single storefront has grown into a leading retailer of commercial and home gym equipment across the United States.

We're proud to offer our own French Fitness brand alongside other premium equipment lines, all backed by our commitment to quality, service, and customer satisfaction.
